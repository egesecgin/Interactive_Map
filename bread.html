Project Title
“soba” is a surrealistic installation built out of the remnants of the building and makes the dynamics of this unique space visible. It is set up in front of the fascinating window, that connects the building to the nature outside, the ocean. To enhance the strong gravitational pull to the exterior, flakes of the walls are used and hang on the ceiling with a transparent, elastic thread. While closer to the walls more flakes are installed, they get less dense towards the center. This spread imitates an impression of dissolving wall-parts into open space and unfolds borders. While the wind blows, the hanging wall-pieces make a singular, subtle sound and causes a slight feeling of satisfaction, peace and freedom.
